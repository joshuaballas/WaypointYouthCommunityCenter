Meet Our Team

At the heart of Waypoint are those who serve. Here are a few of those who have dedicated their lives to this mission.

Chris Morse

Executive Director

Chris lives with his wife, Pam, in Forward Township, PA. He has been serving as the worship leader and the Creative Arts Director at House of Faith in West Newton, PA for the past 20 years, and also is an Associate Pastor there. He worked as a carpenter for 25 years until God called him into full-time ministry. He is also the Director of Worship for the Coalition of Fellowship. Chris heads up Waypoint Worship, has a mentoring group of 21 young adults, and works day-to-day at the youth center.

Pam Morse

President

Pam lives in Forward Township, PA with her husband, Chris. Pam works full time at PricewaterhouseCoopers as a Director in the Wealth Management Tax Services group. She is always at Waypoint events, and is a mentor alongside her husband to 21 young adults.

Justin Lowanse

Community Liaison

Justin Lowanse lives in West Newton, PA. Justin works full time at Bella Construction as a Sales Representative but has dedicated all of his free time to Waypoint. Justin is a reliable leader, not only at Waypoint but also in West Newton. He is a passionate, hard worker and a great ally to the Waypoint team.

Jalena Morse

Program Coordinator

Jalena lives in West Newton, PA with her husband, Declan. She moved from South Dakota in January, 2021 to be a part of Waypoint and the community of West Newton. She works as the Program Coordinator of Waypoint and serves on the worship team at House of Faith.

Declan Morse

Executive Coordinator

Declan lives in West Newton, PA with his wife Jalena. Declan has been working at Waypoint since 2018 and moved to West Newton in 2020. He works as the Executive Coordinator of Waypoint and serves on the worship team at House of Faith.

Mallory Crawford

Dietary Coordinator

Mallory lives in West Newton, PA with her husband Joseph and their two dogs. She is passionate about the importance of the community and sharing ideas over a good meal. When not at Waypoint, you can find her working at the local brewery or next door to the Outpost and volunteering with Downtown West Newton Inc.

Gloria Walden

Dietary Coordinator

Gloria lives in Liberty Boro, PA with her husband Carl. She has been a kitchen mom for Waypoint since 2018. She worked as a Respiratory Therapist for 22 years at Forbes Regional Hospital and is now retired. She also was a youth leader in her late 20's. She has two boys, Luke and Clay.
